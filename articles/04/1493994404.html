Australia bushfires: Forest destruction captured in aerial photos

Ripu Bhatia10:12, Jan 14 2020
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Whats App
  • Reddit
  • Email

New images have captured the impact of bushfires on Australia’s landscape.

About 8.4 million hectares of land has burned across the continent since the fire season started in July.

The fires are some of the worst seen in decades, killing 27 people and more than one billion animals and destroying thousands of homes.

Now, mapping company Nearmap has released before-and-after aerial shots illustrating the scale of the damage.
