Watertown man heading to prison for selling cocaine

January 27, 2020 at 11:50 AM EST - Updated January 27 at 11:50 AM

LOWVILLE, N.Y. (WWNY) - A Watertown man who admitted to selling cocaine in the town of Denmark was sentenced to six years in prison.

Twenty-nine year old Xavier Rose pleaded guilty in November to third-degree criminal sale of a controlled substance.

He was sentenced in Lewis County Court on Friday.

Rose was also sentenced to three years’ probation.
