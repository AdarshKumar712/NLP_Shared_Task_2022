Hank — Petraits Rescue

Tweet
By ChicagoNow Staff, Wednesday at 7:57 pm
Hank is a six-year-old, 45-pound, male purebred Shar Pei looking for a loving guardian.
 
What this guy lacks in the looks department, he more than makes up with his personality.
 
He had a severe skin infection that left his mostly bald and looking like a Mexican hairless dog mixed with a sloth.
 
Hank is a total love with all people and his tail is constantly wagging. He is also great with other dogs, but is sometimes food aggressive based on his sad neglected past. Found stray in Missouri, he’s lucky he made his way to Pei Land Rescue in Plano, Illinois.
 
Hank is on his way to a full recovery, neutered, de-wormed, up-to-date on vaccines, micro-chipped and heartworm-tested negative.
 
His adoption fee of $250 benefits the rescued pets of Pei Land Rescue.
 
To meet and possibly adopt Hank, please contact Jen at peachesash@aol.com or 630-553-7829.
