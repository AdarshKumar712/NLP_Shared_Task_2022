My wishlist for 2020

January 2nd, 2020 | Author: Contributions

1. Delinking PSLE school result certificate to unpaid school fees by parents so our children will have the dignity of receiving what is due to them

2. Implementation of a minimum wage for the low-income earners so our people can have the dignity of earning a decent wage for the hard work put in than constantly relying on hand-out

3. Our people will be able to voice out their grievances courageously despite the threat of suppression as that is the only way to win over tyranny

4. That we all see ourselves as one people – Singaporeans – not Chinese, Malay, Indian or Eurasian so that we can stay united and support one another despite our differences

5. Provide employment for all Singaporeans first before approving further work permit unless it’s a job that no Singaporean can perform

6. Limit the duration of a Prime Minister to a maximum of two terms so there is a proper succession plan

7. Singaporeans to vote in sufficient number of opposition to provide a check and balance to the incumbent so that no laws and legislation can be easily allowed through without a proper debate

8. The opposition finally unite and form a coalition to take on the incumbent in the coming election

9. A proper pension plan in place to take care of our elderly folks after they retire – currently too many of them toil in low-end jobs trying to survive

10. That national service will be further reduced to 12 months and the army gradually evolve into a professional one so our young men can have a good head-start in their study or career

 

Gilbert Goh
