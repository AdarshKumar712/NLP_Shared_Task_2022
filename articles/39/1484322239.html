    Sevilla train with full compliment ahead of Athletic Club clash

    LaLiga No injuries for Lopetegui

      • A. F.
      • Sevilla
      • Adapted by Billy Munday
      • Compartir en Facebook
      • Compartir en Twitter
      • Enviar por email
    • 01/01/2020 22:30 CET
    En Español

    Julen Lopetegui had his entire first team squad available as Sevilla trained on Wednesday afternoon ahead of their match with Athletic Club on Sunday.

    Nolito trained having missed the last three outings of 2019 with injury.

    Sevilla president Jose Castro and sporting director Monchi were also at the club's training ground to support Lopetegui and his players.

    The squad will hold an open training session at 11:30 CET on Thursday at the Estadio Roman Sanchez Pizjuan.

    Away from the pitch, Monchi is looking into new signings in the January transfer window.
