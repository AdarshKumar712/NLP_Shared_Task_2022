Cochrane-Fountain City employee fired after sending nude photos to student

Local News
Posted: Feb 28, 2020 / 11:06 AM CST / Updated: Feb 28, 2020 / 11:06 AM CST

Cochrane- Fountain City School District Superintendent Michele Butler says the school district has terminated Heather Treague’s employment following an investigation into nude photos sent to a student.

According to a criminal complaint, Heather Treague, 34, allegedly sent 10 nude photos over Snapchat to a student. These photos were allegedly sent outside of school hours.

Treague’s signature bond has been set at $5,000 and she is not to have contact with any minors other than her own. She is scheduled to appear in court next on March, 4.
