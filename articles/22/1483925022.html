Montreal weather: Snow early, then mainly cloudy

Periods of light snow will end in the morning with a west wind of 20 km/h, gusting to 40.  

Montreal Gazette
Updated: January 1, 2020
Share Adjust Comment Print

Periods of light snow will end in the morning with a west wind of 20 km/h, gusting to 40.

Environment Canada is calling for a high of minus 1.

Tonight: Cloudy with a low of minus 3.
