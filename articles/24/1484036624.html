Merced County man accused of groping a teenager in her bed, Gustine police say

Sun-Star staff

January 01, 2020 07:45 AM
    ORDER REPRINT →

A 37-year-old Gustine man was arrested Tuesday after police said he groped a teenage family member in her bed.

The 17-year-old victim told police that Ruben Lucio Flores Jr. climbed into her bed, grabbed her body while he was aroused, the Gustine Police Department said in a statement posted online.

The alleged victim reported the incident at 6:36 a.m. Tuesday. Lucio Flores was arrested within the hour, police said.

He was booked into the Merced County Jail on suspicion of sexual battery, a felony. He was not listed as an inmate Wednesday morning.
