Exxon Mobil Corporation Declares Second Quarter Dividend

The Board of Directors of Exxon Mobil Corporation (NYSE:XOM) today declared a cash dividend of $0.
Author:
Business Wire
Publish date:
Apr 29, 2020 4:39 PM EDT

The Board of Directors of Exxon Mobil Corporation (XOM) - Get Report today declared a cash dividend of $0.87 cents per share on the Common Stock, payable on June 10, 2020 to shareholders of record of Common Stock at the close of business on May 13, 2020.

This second quarter dividend is at the same level as the dividend paid in the first quarter of 2020.

Through its dividends, the corporation has shared its success with its shareholders for more than 100 years.
