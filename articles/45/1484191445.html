Westerly kindergarten registration begins Jan. 6

  • Dec 20, 2019
  • Dec 20, 2019 Updated 5 hrs ago

WESTERLY — Kindergarten registration for the school year beginning September 2020 will start Monday, Jan. 6. Children must be five years of age prior to Sept. 1, 2020 to be eligible for kindergarten entry.

Registration packets can be downloaded and printed directly from the student registration page found on the Westerly Public Schools website. Families also have the option of picking up a registration packet at Babcock Hall, 23 Highland Ave.; Tower Street School Community Center, 93 Tower St.; or at a neighborhood Westerly elementary school.

Families must bring the following documents to their personal appointment: completed registration packet; child’s birth certificate; child’s current physical and immunization records; and family proof of residency in the form of a Westerly tax bill, utility bill, lease or mortgage statement. An affidavit of residency will be notarized during the appointment. All documents must be presented at the time of the registration appointment.

Families should contact Christine Miller at 401-315-1509 to schedule an appointment time. Families unable to obtain all the required forms should advise Miller at the time of booking an appointment. The student registration office is located at Babcock Hall. Walk-ins cannot be accommodated.
