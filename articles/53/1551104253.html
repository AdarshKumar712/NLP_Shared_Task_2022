New Facebook Group Supports Tri-City Business During COVID

John McKay
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

A new Facebook group is rapidly growing in the Tri-Cities, and it's for a great cause.

Tri-Cities Washington Support Local During COVID-19 is intended to call attention to, and help generate support and business for many small area establishments. From restaurants to digital/video outfits, from clothing makers to lawn care, the page helps expose mom-and-pop outfits (many you've heard of) to survive this economic hardship due to Coronavirus.
