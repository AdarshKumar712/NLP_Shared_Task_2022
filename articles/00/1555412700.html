A new Facebook group named Cyprus Covid-19 Pandemic Helpers has been formed to provide a platform to those businesses and individuals who want to share the services they can offer to those most in need in the coming weeks.

“This group has been established to become a focal point across Cyprus for all persons, business, other groups to post what they can offer, to whom and where and also to ask for help,” the organisers said.
