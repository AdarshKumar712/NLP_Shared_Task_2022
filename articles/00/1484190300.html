East Austin woman nearly struck by bullet amid NYE celebratory gunfire, police say

 
Mary Huber @marymhuber

Wednesday

Jan 1, 2020 at 3:00 PM


A woman was nearly struck in the head after a bullet entered her East Austin home just after midnight New Year’s Eve, an Austin police officer said.

Senior police officer Bino Cadenas said Wednesday on Twitter that a bullet came through the woman’s roof at her home off China Rose Drive and almost hit her while she was sitting on her sofa.

He posted two photos online: one of a hole in the woman’s ceiling and another of a bullet sitting on the kitchen counter.

“Please, please.. don’t shoot up in the air!” Cadenas said. “It’s irresponsible, illegal, and dangerous.”

In Houston, a woman was fatally shot just after midnight Wednesday while standing outside her home, the Harris County Sheriff’s Office said. Philippa Ashford, 61, who worked as a nurse manager at The Menninger Clinic, died from what officials said was likely celebratory gunfire.
