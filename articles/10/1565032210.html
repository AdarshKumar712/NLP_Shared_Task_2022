FRANKFURT — Commerzbank will not extend the right to name soccer team Eintracht Frankfurt’s home stadium after the end of June 2020 when it expires after 15 years, it said in a press release on Wednesday.

The club has decided to engage in a sponsorship deal with Commerzbank rival Deutsche Bank, Deutsche Bank confirmed. (Reporting by Vera Eckert, editing by Thomas Seythal)
