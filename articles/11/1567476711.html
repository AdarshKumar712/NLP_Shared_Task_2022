Today’s Forecast: April 3, 2020

April 3, 2020
Daniel Johnson,

Temperatures will drop into the mid 40s tonight with mostly cloudy skies and slightly breezy winds.

Saturday will consist of mostly cloudy skies and slightly cooler temperatures in the mid 50s. Sunday will be partly sunny and seasonable with milder highs in the low 60s.

Monday will be mostly sunny and pleasant with highs reaching the upper 60s. Partly sunny skies are in the forecast on Tuesday with highs in the upper 60s to lower 70s. An isolated shower is possible.

Temperatures will stay in the upper 60s to lower 70s on Wednesday with partly sunny skies and isolated showers. The partly sunny skies and isolated showers continue into Thursday with highs in the mid 60s.

Friday will be mostly sunny and cooler with highs falling back into the 50s.

Have a great weekend!

-Daniel
