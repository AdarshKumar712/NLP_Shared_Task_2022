Chidambaram criticises Delhi govt decision to prosecute Kanhaiya in sedition case

Senior Congress leader and former finance minister P Chidambaram said he "strongly disapproved" the Delhi government's decision to prosecute former JNUSU president Kanhaiya Kumar and nine others in the 2016 JNU sedition case.¬†

Senior Congress leader P Chidambaram (L) and Kanhaiya Kumar. (File)

Senior Congress leader and former finance minister P Chidambaram Saturday criticised the Arvind Kejriwal-led Delhi government for giving the go-ahead to prosecute former JNUSU president Kanhaiya Kumar and nine others in the 2016 JNU sedition case. He said he “strongly disapproved” the decision.
