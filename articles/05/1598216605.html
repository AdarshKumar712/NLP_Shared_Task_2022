COVID cases in Germany rises to 164,807, death toll nears 7,000

Berlin, May 6 (UNI) Germany on Wednesday confirmed 947 fresh cases of novel coronavirus over the last 24 hours, taking the total tally to 164,807 while the overall death toll soared to 6996.

A total of 165 people succumbed to the fatal virus in that period. The country has so far seen around 1,37,000 recoveries.

The highest number of COVID-19 cases has been recorded in Bavaria (43,371), North Rhine-Westphalia (33,977) and Baden-Wurttemberg (32,576). Berlin has 6,092 COVID-19 cases.
