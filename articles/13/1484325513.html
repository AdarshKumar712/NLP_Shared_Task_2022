The peaks and ridges of Mount Adams' summit are illuminated against a pink-hued sunset. This scene was photographed from the treeline of Cascade Peaks in Mount St. Helens National Volcanic Monument during what photographers refer to as "golden hour" or "magic hour," a short window of time after sunrise and before sunset. During these intervals the sun is higher or lower in the sky compared to afternoon daylight which, in good weather conditions, provides a softer natural light desirable to landscape and portrait photographers. The above image was captured at 8:48 p.m. with a Nikon D5 camera at a 200 mm focal length.

From the tulip field in Woodland to the pumice plain of Mount St. Helens, Daily News photographers captured thousands of images in 2019. These are the moments that defined the last 365 days in Cowlitz County, separated by month.

January

In January 2019 the community rallied for women's rights, mourned the death of a Kayla Chapman and said farewell to Daily News photographer Bill Wagner, who worked for TDN for 32 years. See all Daily News top January photos here.

February

In February 2019 a Woodland woman finally buried her brother, who died in Pearl Harbor, and people of all ages rallied against fracked gas at the Washington State Capitol. See all Daily News top February photos here.

March

In March 2019 Cowlitz County residents and responders fought wildfires, the community funded a Diabetic alert dog for a Kalama kid and Spring sports heated up. See all Daily News top March photos here.

April

In April 2019 Cowlitz County mourned slain sheriff's deputy Justin DeRosier and a Castle Rock High School student built her own house. See all Daily News top April photos here.

May

In May 2019 a Silver Lake man was reunited with his late wife's wedding ring and the Kalama Police Department unveiled its new station. See all Daily News top May photos here.

June 

In June 2019 local high school students celebrated their graduation, a Longview native founded an anti-sexual assault group and the community soaked up every bit of summer. See all Daily News top June photos here.

In July 2019 thousands flocked to summer festivals all over the county and a Longview child saved a her neighbor's life by alerting her father of a house fire. See all Daily News top July photos here.

September

In September 2019 Cowlitz County honored first responders and veterans at annual Day of Caring event and a Winlock woman visited every city in Cowlitz County in passport challenge. See all Daily News top September photos here.

October

In October 2019 the community remembered Cowlitz 2 Fire & Rescue Battalion Chief Mickel Zainfeld, a Castle Rock family transformed their barn into a Halloween attraction to raise money for their daughter and a heated feud over homeless shelter options broke out during a Longview community forum. See all Daily News top October photos here.

November

In December 2019 two Vietnam veterans reunited in Longview and part of the community rallied in support of President Donald Trump's impeachment. See all Daily News top December photos here.
