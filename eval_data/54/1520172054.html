What’s in the stars this week?

byCaroline Nihill
February 12, 2020
3 min read

ARIES: This week marks a string of compassionate days, Aries. Something’s on your mind and it’s not leaving until you’ve settled the argument either internally or externally. Take a moment and breathe. Last week might’ve been the time to listen, but now you’re ready to act. Make time to do some deciding this week. 

TAURUS: Taurus, normally the flow of your life involves long deliberations and small time for action. This may be increasingly true this week, or maybe it’s the opposite. Spend some time asking yourself whether or not the path you’re on right now is right for you. If not, why do you want it to work so badly?

GEMINI: If you’re in a planning mood, dear Gemini, now is definitely the time to execute a weekend trip, a dinner or any other social gathering. Your extroverted tendencies are calling, and ignoring it will do you no good. Don’t shut yourself inside out of fear; you should ride this out and see whatever is making you social until it’s end. 

CANCER: Are you grappling for something new, Cancer? Maybe things are getting shaken up in your personal life. While change isn’t always good, this might be a good time to fully embrace it. Remember your foundations, but don’t be afraid to take a stroll on the wild side here or there. 

LEO: If whatever lies ahead of you isn’t truly what your heart desires, why are you so interested in pursuing it? Maybe it’s the need to change the game for whatever reason. Is this something you truly want? Your immediate answer might be a hard ‘no,’ but with some thought, you might be able to see what aspect of your option is so alluring. 

VIRGO: Virgo, right now you may feel like you’re driver’s side to an adventure comparable to your dreams. This isn’t a bad thing! In fact, it’s good to let go and enjoy what life has to offer. Just a warning – you may want to check to make sure the adventure isn’t rose-colored before you commit to anything serious. 

LIBRA: If you’re feeling lost this week, Libra, don’t fret; the sky will clear soon (despite the weather forecasts). Maybe you’re stuck on something or don’t know what your next decision will be. Some friendly advice: think a bit more on the subject. Don’t think for too long, though. Inaction is also action.

SCORPIO: Are you leaning on someone, Scorpio? This week may feel like the time to ask for help from others, but make sure you’re also listening to yourself. It can be easy to let other people take the reins, but you also need to trust your gut in this situation. Familiarity breeds contempt. 

SAGITTARIUS: It’s easy to get fired up about something, Sagittarius. Don’t mistake being hyped up for being passionate. Emotions like anger, sadness and apathy can get the best of us sometimes. It’s important to hit the breaks and look around. Are you focusing on the devil in the details? Or just the details?

CAPRICORN: Capricorn, all is not quiet this week. While you may be watching something to make sure nothing chaotic happens, it may be that rigidity isn’t your friend either. It’s time to trust your gut and express yourself. If not, venting is the other way to alleviate these hypervigilant feelings. 

AQUARIUS: Some days, Aquarius, it might feel like you’re speaking another language than your friends. If you’re trying to avoid calamity at all costs, this isn’t the answer. Take the time to sit down, and sound out what you’re trying to explain. There’s no need to get up in arms over a little miscommunication. 

PISCES: While you may not be a huge fan of delayed gratification, Pisces, it may be the best thing for you right now. Life doesn’t always go with your flow, and that’s okay. Time to adjust and look at the big picture; it’ll help you figure things out.
