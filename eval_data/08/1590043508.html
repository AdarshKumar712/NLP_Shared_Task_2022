How To Original Stitch

Now, everyone can wear custom.

Step 1

Select a Design

Start with a style, color or pattern from our curated collections–or begin fresh to design your own–starting at just $49. Now, everyone can wear custom.

Step 2

Customize You Shirt

Select your custom styles and fabrics for your one-of-a-kind shirt.

Step 3

Use Fit Algorithm

Tell us a few of your measurements, as well as fit preference (Slim, Regular, Relaxed) to enable made to measure, custom tailored shirting like you've never worn before.

Step 4

Place Order

Original Stitch shirts are carefully handcrafted at our factory in Japan, and ship worldwide for delivery in 15 days. Any issues with your shirt, and we'll remake it free, for 30 days.
