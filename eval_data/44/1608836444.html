United States likely to resume 10% finding to WHO

Updated : 17 May 2020 09:51 AM (IST)

United States President Donald Trump has taken a U-turn over his policies regarding WHO funding. President Trump had earlier stopped the funding to the World Health Organisation claiming that WHO is siding with China over the COVID-19 issue. Now, Donald Trump has announced on his twitter handle that he is thinking of many proposals which include the resumption of 10 percent funding to the World Health Organisation.
