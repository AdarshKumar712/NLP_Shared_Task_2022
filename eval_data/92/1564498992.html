Iran to rejoin JCPOA? Putin and Merkel working now.

Share:Twitter Facebook LinkedIn


“Iran will come back to JCPOA,” is what Putin believes.

According to official statements issued by the Kremlin, (Translated), “Vladimir Putin and Angela Merkel plan to discuss bilateral cooperation and relevant topics on the international agenda: the situation in Syria, Libya, the escalation of tension in the Middle East and the problems of resolving the intra-Ukrainian conflict in the context of the implementation of the Minsk “Package of Measures” of 2015 and the agreements reached at the summit in “Norman format” December 9, 2019 in Paris.”
