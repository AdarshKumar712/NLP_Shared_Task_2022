Netherlands to face Italy and Poland in UEFA Nations League

Posted by admin on March 3rd, 2020 06:24 PM | News, Oranje  

The group draw for the second edition of the UEFA Nations League took place on Tuesday with Netherlands drawn into a group with Italy, Poland and Bosnia and Herzegovina.

  • Follow Football-Oranje on Twitter

Netherlands had a great tournament during the first Nations League. After topping their group ahead of France and Germany, Ronald Koeman’s side beat England, before going down to Portugal in the final.

In the second edition of the tournament, Netherlands will face Italy, Bosnia & Herzegovina and Poland.

The first round of games will be played between the between the 3rd and 5th of September 2020.
