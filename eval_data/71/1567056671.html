Hoosier musician Clayton Anderson performing virtual concert

Clayton Anderson to perform virtual concert

by: McKinzie Roth
Posted: Apr 2, 2020 / 05:15 PM EST / Updated: Apr 2, 2020 / 06:05 PM EST

INDIANAPOLIS (WISH) — News 8’s Entertainment Insider McKinzie Roth talked Thursday on “All Indiana: with country musician Clayton Anderson.

Anderson, who is from Indiana, began a virtual concert at 6 p.m. Thursday for a good cause.

Anderson is performing at The Pavilion at Pan Am in downtown Indianapolis to raise money for local bartenders and service industry workers affected by COVID-19. Watch the show and donate on Anderson’s Facebook page. Zink Distributing Co. and Anheuser-Busch will provide matching donations.
