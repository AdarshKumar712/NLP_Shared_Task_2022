Argentina U23 team rumored eleven vs. Chile in Olympic qualifier

By
Roy Nemer
-
January 24, 2020
6
Facebook
Twitter
WhatsApp

    Argentina will look to get their second win following their 2-1 victory vs. Colombia.

    Fernando Batista will be without Alexis Mac Allister after the goal scorer received a red card in the first game. His replacement, Matias Zaracho, is now also a doubt for the game vs. Chile.

    Per a report by TyC Sports, should Zaracho not start vs. Colombia, it will be Nahuel Bustos who would replace him. Here’s the rumored eleven:

    Facundo Cambeses; Marcelo Herrera, Nahuen Perez, Facundo Medina, Claudio Bravo; Fausto Vera, Nicolas Capaldo, Matias Zaracho or Nahuel Bostos; Julian Alvarez, Agustin Urzi, Adolfo Gaich
