It’s amazing how some things don’t seem to change...

Letters to the Editor

Published Jan 27, 2020 at 8:00 am (Updated Jan 27, 2020 at 8:09 am)

  • Gray Malin (Photograph supplied)


Related Stories

  • Bermudian boy features in BBC story on racism

Dear Sir,

I just looked at the collection of photographs, having followed the links from RG Mag.

What I noticed is that there is not a single non-white person in Gray Malin’s photos!

Amazing!

This perpetuates the mentality and the reality I knew when I lived there, although I’m reading these days in your paper about efforts towards further change and resolution of the racial divide.

Come on, people, it’s been 64 years since I left Bermuda!

YVONNE D. MILLINGTON

Saint Lambert, Quebec

Canada
