Trump: No Question China Misled the World on the Coronavirus

‘We wanted to go in, they didn’t want to us go in early’
News & Politics, International, Science & Nature

RUSH EXCERPT:

TRUMP: “I don’t think there is any question about it. We wanted to go in, they didn’t want to us go in early. I don’t think there is any question. China tried to blame it on some of our soldiers that turned out to not go too far. I got very upset with that. Then they tried to blame it on Europe. I said how did Europe get involved. Italy suffered more than anybody per capita. Spain, France is on additional lockdown. All of Europe. It’s been a Disaster.”
