Costa Blanca News: Madrid debut for Alfaz’s Film Festival

By
Linda Hall
-
27 January 2020 @ 13:57
0


ALFAZ presented its Welcome l’Alfas brand and this year’s Film Festival poster at the Fitur tourism trade fair in Madrid. Alfaz’s mayor Vicente Arques explained that the Welcome l’Alfas initiative is based on sustainable and healthful urban development in a municipality where 52% of the population come from 102 different countries and live in a town with an incalculable natural heritage.
Fitur also provided the ideal occasion for presenting the poster, publicising Alfaz’s Film Festival, converting the Madrid tourism fair as the first official event in the festival’s 32nd edition, Arques said.
“This is the ideal setting for it,” the mayor added.
