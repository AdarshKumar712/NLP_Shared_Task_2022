Muhyiddin receives expression of support from Cuepacs

The Congress of Unions of Employees in the Public and Civil Services (Cuepacs) expressed its unwavering confidence and support in newly-appointed Prime Minister Tan Sri Muhyiddin Yassin. BERNAMA pic
By Bernama - March 1, 2020 @ 1:33pm

KUALA LUMPUR: The Congress of Unions of Employees in the Public and Civil Services (Cuepacs) expressed its unwavering confidence and support in newly-appointed Prime Minister Tan Sri Muhyiddin Yassin.

Its president, Adnan Mat, said civil servants always work with the government of the day to move the country towards excellence, and provide effective and quality services to the people and the country.

“Throughout several changes of governance, the civil service has remained steadfast in its neutrality to continue its services in looking after the wellbeing of the people under the purview of the royal institution.

“Therefore, Cuepacs and civil servants will continue to provide quality and effective services to the people and the country on the basis of loyalty to Yang-Pertuan Agong Al-Sultan Abdullah Ri’ayatuddin Al-Mustafa Billah Shah,” he said in a statement today.

Muhyiddin, who is also president of Parti Pribumi Bersatu Malaysia, was sworn in as the 8th Prime Minister before the Yang di-Pertuan Agong at Istana Negara today. – BERNAMA
