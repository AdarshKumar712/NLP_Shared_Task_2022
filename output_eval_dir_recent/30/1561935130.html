He goes into detail explaining that the lungs are a pair of spongy, air-filled organs located on either side of the chest (thorax). Also that the lungs are covered by a thin tissue layer called the pleura a thin layer of fluid which acts as a lubricant allowing the lungs to slip smoothly as they expand and contract with each breath.
