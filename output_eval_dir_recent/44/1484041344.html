Rudy Giuliani’s Ukraine fixers arrested over donations

Boer Deng, Washington

October 11 2019, 12:01am, The Times

Two close associates of President Trump’s personal lawyer have been charged with funnelling foreign cash into a campaign backing his election.

Lev Parnas and Igor Fruman, American citizens born in the Soviet Union, and described as aides of Rudy Giuliani, were arrested on Wednesday at Washington international airport as they prepared to leave the United States on a one-way ticket. They had been scheduled to speak to the House of Representatives impeachment inquiry yesterday and today.

A federal indictment alleges that they channelled $325,000 to a pro-Trump fund, America First Action, through a front company to disguise the origin of the money, which was from a person described in the indictment as “Foreign National-1”. It is illegal for foreign citizens to fund US politicians.
