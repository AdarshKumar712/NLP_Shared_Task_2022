    Omet appoints agent in Mexico

    Manroland Latina will help Omet strengthen its presence in the region.

    Pedro Huerta (L), Hans Ramon Hofmann and Cesar Dominguez of Manroland Latina with an Omet X6 machine.
    Related CONTENT
    • DIP Co expands offering for Omet XFlex line
    • Labelexpo Europe 2019 set for largest show to date
    • Sun Chemical touts SunPak FSP inks
    • Mumbai converter adds third Omet press
    • Omet launches new XFlex X7
    01.09.20
    Omet has announced its collaboration with a new agent in Mexico, Manroland Latina, with the target of increasing its presence in the area and being closer to local printers.

    Omet is experiencing global growth with the installation of narrow and medium web printing machines for labels and flexible packaging on all continents. With the goal of growing and strengthening its presence in Mexico, Omet has signed the collaboration agreement. The German-based company has been supplying the country with printing machines and related graphic arts equipment and consumables for more than 60 years.

    César Domínguez, general manager of Manroland Latina, says, “Omet is a leader in the narrow web and mid web labels and packaging business, providing the best available solutions to the market. We are thrilled to create a new business unit for web fed labels and packaging in order to complete our present offering. We feel that their machines satisfy our very demanding requirements for quality and productivity. Both companies, Manroland Sheetfed and Omet share the same customer approach philosophy.”

    Hans Ramón Hofmann, manager of the newly created business unit, adds, “I am really excited to get back to this beloved market with exceptional quality and technology machines. We will be able to compete at the highest level in this very demanding market with a low total cost of ownership and an excellent value for money product.

    Manroland Latina is a subsidiary in Mexico for Manroland Sheetfed, with 45 employees located mainly in service, sales of equipment and consumables and administration. The company has a presence in Mexico City, Guadalajara and Monterrey.
