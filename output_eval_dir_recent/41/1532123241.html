Feeling Feverish? The List of 101 Designated Fever Clinics in Beijing

The Beijingers | Feb 8, 2020 9:00 am | 1 comment | 773 reads

If you have a fever, you must go to one of these fever clinics as designated by the People's Government of Beijing Municipality. Going to just any hospital or clinic will result in you being referred to one of the below (AKA being turned away). So better not waste time and go straight to a designated fever clinic.

If you use Baidu Maps, an easy way to find the nearest one to your current location is to do a search in Chinese for 发热门诊 (fārè ménzhěn, fever clinic). We have highlighted some of the more common/popular foreign-friendly institutions.
