North Ayrshire's senior orchestra donate £500 to foodbank

By Andy Hamilton Reporter

North Ayrshire music friends donate £500 to foodbank

0 comment

The Foodbank was given a kind donation after coronavirus cancelled North Ayrshire’s senior orchestra and concert bands’s Summer tour.

Friends of North Ayrshire Music donated £500 last week after North Ayrshire Foodbank had to put out an urgent appeal for support during the current crisis.

Funds were raised by members over Christmas but with the Barcelona tour now postponed until next year, the funds were donated instead.

A North Ayrshire Council spokesperson said: “Thank you all for your generosity and choosing instead to help support the Foodbank during this difficult time.”
