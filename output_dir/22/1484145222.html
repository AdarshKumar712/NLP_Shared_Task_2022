Inside Anfield: Reds close 2019 out with Wolves win

Glenn Price @GlennPrice94

Take a behind-the-scenes look at Liverpool's victory over Wolverhampton Wanderers with the latest edition of Inside Anfield.

Sadio Mane's first-half goal on Sunday was enough to see the Reds go the entireity of 2019 unbeaten at home, while restoring their 13-point lead at the top of the Premier League.

Our cameras were granted exclusive access inside the ground before, during and after the match - and you can watch the footage they captured in the video below.
