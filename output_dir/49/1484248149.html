Honorable Prime Minister Silveria E. Jacobs New Year’s Address. | SMN News

January 1, 2020

PHILIPSBURG:— Whether you will be heading to a church, to a party, or bringing in the New Year in the comfort of your own home, New Years is a time to reflect and set goals, and as we say goodbye to this year, I would like to take this opportunity to reflect on 2019 for the Government of St. Maarten.

 Fellow St. Maarteners and visitors, on behalf of the Council of Ministers, I’d like to wish each and every one of you a Happy and Prosperous New Year.

 Firstly, I want to Thank God almighty for another year; one without any
