PHOTOS: Jamaicans get baptised, bring in 2020 with worship

Loop News  Created : 1 January 2020

A woman being baptised at New Testament Church of God in Old Harbour,StCatherine on Tuesday evening as worshippers welcomed 2020 at the traditional watchnight service.

The New Testament  Church of God in Old Harbour, St Catherine was packed to capacity Tuesday with worshippers who rang in the new year with the traditional watchnight service.

A major highlight of the night was the baptism of 10 persons - eight females and two males - who responded to a call by Bishop Hugh Dixon to start the new year right by giving their lives to Christ.
