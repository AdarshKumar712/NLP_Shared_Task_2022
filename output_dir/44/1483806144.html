Saudi- Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques Congratulates President of Cuba on Liberation Day


Date

12/31/2019 3:00:50 AM
  • Share on Facebook
  • Tweet on Twitter

(MENAFN- Saudi Press Agency) Riyadh, December 31, 2019, SPA -- The Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques King Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud has sent a cable of congratulations to President Miguel Díaz-Canel of the Republic of Cuba on the anniversary of his country's Liberation Day.
The King wished the President constant good health and happiness and the Government and people of Cuba steady progress and prosperity.
--SPA
