Weather forecast: Rain and snow until Saturday

January 1, 2020 at 9:38am
Edited by Andreas Nicolaides

Today there will be isolated showers island-wide and snow or snowshowers in the mountains. Snow depth in Troodos today is 30 cm.

Temperatures will reach 18 C inland and on the coast and 6 C in the mountains and tonight they will drop to 7 C inland, 10 C on the coast and 0 C in the mountains.

On Thursday, Friday and Saturday there will be local showers and isolated thunderstorms and snow will fall in the mountains.

There will be a slight drop in temperature on Thursday to reach normal levels for the time of year. No significant shifts in temperature are anticipated for Friday and Saturday.
