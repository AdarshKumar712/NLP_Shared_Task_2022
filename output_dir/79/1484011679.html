Parliament and government composition database (ParlGov)

ParlGov is a new data infrastructure for political science. It contains data on elections and governments for all EU and most OECD members from 1945 until today. The database combines approximately 1600 parties, 550 elections (4000 results), and 850 governments (2000 cabinet parties).
