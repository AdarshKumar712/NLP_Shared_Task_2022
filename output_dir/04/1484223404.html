Resigning after 1 yr of service

6 hours ago

Can I resign my job here in Kuwait after I finish 1 year of service?

Name withheld

Answer: Yes you can, if you were hired locally and you don’t possess a university degree. If you however, possess a degree you can put in your resignation at any point of time. On the other hand, if you were brought to Kuwait from your home country by your current sponsor, then that law mandates you to work three years for him/her before you can resign or ask for a release.
