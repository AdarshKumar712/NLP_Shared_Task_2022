Hiro Mashima Teases “Surprising Announcements” for 2020
by Joseph Luster January 1, 2020
SHARE

Now that the new year has arrived, it’s time to look ahead to what our favorite creators have in store for us. Fairy Tail manga author Hiro Mashima already stoked the flames of speculation in his final Twitter post of 2019, in which he teased “many surprising announcements” for 2020.

Mashima currently has two works running in Weekly Shonen Magazine, including Edens Zero and HERO’S. He shared an illustration to go along with his message, and shortly followed it up with a Happy New Year drawing. Check out both below while we wait to hear what’s next for the author.
