Where To Watch Four College Football Bowl Games Set For Today

Rob Kirkpatrick
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

There is a ton of college football bowl action today. Things kick-off with the Outback Bowl in Tampa. Then, the Vrbo Citrus Bowl in Orlando. All eyes then shift to the west coast for the 131st Tournament of Roses. Finally, the Allstate Sugar Bowl kick-off at 7:40 PM CT from the Mercedes-Benz Superdome in New Orleans.

Here is where to watch it all. 

 

  • 1

    Outback Bowl

    Outback Bowl
    (18) Minnesota vs. (12) Auburn
    1:00pm ET | ESPN
    Raymond James Stadium – Tampa, FL

  • 2

    VRBO Citrus Bowl

    Vrbo Citrus Bowl
    (14) Michigan vs. (13) Alabama
    1:00pm ET | ABC
    Camping World Stadium – Orlando, FL

  • 3

    Rose Bowl

    (6) Oregon vs. (8) Wisconsin
    5:00pm ET | ESPN
    Rose Bowl Stadium – Pasadena, CA

  • 4

    Allstate Sugar Bowl

    (5) Georgia vs. (7) Baylor
    8:45pm ET | ESPN
    Mercedes-Benz Superdome – New Orleans, LA
