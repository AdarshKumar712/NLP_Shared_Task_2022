Saboteurs face Cottesmore Hunt supporters at New Year's Day Meet in Melton

A group of around 30 anti-hunt campaigners were present along with hundreds of supporters

Hunt saboteurs tried to disrupt the tradition New Years Day on Melton Mowbray today.

A large crowd of hunt supporters turned up to watch members of the Cottesmore Hunt ride out at the towns Play Close to mark the start of 2020.

However there was also a group of around 50 anti-hunt campaigners who arrived from across the country.

They were carrying placards and some wore masks as they staged a vocal protest.

One observer told LeicestershireLive: Ive been coming to the New Years Day meet for years and this is the first time there have ever been sabs that I know of.

There wasnt any trouble but they were very vocal and they had some really offensive things written on their placards which wasnt great considering there were so many children there.
