In an effort to keep the roads safe on New Year's Eve, one company in the Valley is offering to pay for your ride.

PHOENIX (3TV/CBS 5) -- If you plan on drinking Tuesday as we bid 2019 adieu, plan ahead because New Year's Eve is notorious for DUI arrests.

[WATCH: Free sober rides available on New Year's Eve]

To help keep Valley residents going into 2020, Valley Metro is teaming up with Coors Light Free Rides to offer transportation at no cost to riders.

Free rides on Valley Metro bus, light rail and paratransit services start at 7 p.m. and go until the end of service. Valley Metro says the last train will leave around 2:30 a.m. Most buses across the Valley operate until about midnight.

As you make your plans for #NYE2020, make sure you youre ready to #celebratesafe. You can ride bus, light rail and Paratransit for FREE starting tonight at 7 p.m. Take advantage of these great public transit options, and have fun tonight! https://t.co/VUCskBa7IZ

— Greg Stanton (@RepGregStanton) December 31, 2019

A Scottsdale-based residential real estate company is doing what it can to help New Year's revelers in its area, too. The Joffe Group is offering to pay for people's cab, Uber, and Lyft rides.

"The first 50 people who send us their bill, we will pay their bill," company spokeswoman Amy Joffe said. "And if the response is overwhelming, we will do our best to pay for anybody we receive a bill from."

The Joffe Group will reimburse you up to $20 plus a 10% tip for your ride on New Year's Eve. The only catch is you have to be coming or going from specific areas. "I would like to eventually be able to do it for the entire city, but right now, we put some perimeters on it -- Arcadia, Scottsdale, and Paradise Valley," Joffe said.

To get reimbursed, mail a photocopy of your valid driver's license, a receipt (or screenshot), and the email associated with your PayPal account to The Joffe Group, 4167 North Marshall Way, Scottsdale, AZ 85251.

Lyft, the Maricopa County Attorney's Office, Desert Financial Credit Union, and Hubbard Radio partnered to offer discounted rides for the holiday season.

New Lyft Riders can use the code JOY for up to $5 off four rides. Existing Lyft customers can use the code JOY19 to get 20% off two trips. You can download the app now and use the codes anytime from now until 11:59 p.m. Wednesday, Jan. 1, 2020.

Join MCAO and @Lyft this holiday season to #SaveLivesDontDUI. Plan for a designated driver to and from your festivities and well give you discounted rides from Lyft.Learn more at: https://t.co/uwj79mX4o1 pic.twitter.com/ehG6FpDuxL

— MaricopaCounty Atty. (@marcoattorney) December 7, 2019
